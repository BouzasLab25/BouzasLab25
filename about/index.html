About

Lab25 is an important research center in Cognitive and Behavioural Sciences, located in the Central Campus of the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), in Mexico City.

Our main interest is to study the Adaptability of Behaviour of human and non human organisms to environmental changes.

More specifically, our areas of interest are:

  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Bayesian modeling
  • Intertemporal choice
  • Risky choice
  • Perceptual decission making
  • Timing
  • Game Theory
  • Categorization
