About

Specialist in Applied Statistics (Research Institute in Applied Mathematics and Systems, UNAM), and B.A. in Psychology from UNAM.

The theoretical topic that I'm interested in the most is intertemporal choice, especially, attribute-based models. However, right now I'm more dedicated in applying Behavioral Sciences in social relevant problems. I'm also interested in decision models, Bayesian cognitive modeling, data analysis, learning models, among others, check out my cv to see more information of what am I doing right now. Cheers!
